RATSCREW

Game Rules

–First player to collect all 52 cards wins

–Each player starts out with 26 cards and takes turns playing (usually) one card at a time from his or her hand into the main deck.

–First player that slaps the main deck when two of the same card are played in a row, gets to add the middle pile to the bottom of their hand. (for example: two 2's, or two Jacks in a row)

–If a player slaps the main deck illegally, they are penalized by losing two cards into the bottom of the main deck. Turn order resumes as it should.

–If a player plays a Jack, the other player must play 1 card. If that card is a Queen, King, or Ace, then the rule restarts for that face card. If the card played is a Jack (two Jacks in a row), slap is allowed. If that card was 2-10, the person who played the Jack gets the main deck.

–If a player plays a Queen, the other player must play 2 cards. If any of those two cards are a face card (Jack, Queen, King, or Ace), then the rule restarts for that face card. Note, if the card was a Queen (two Queens in a row), then it's slappable. If both cards played were 2-10, the person who played the Queen gets the main deck.

–If a player plays a King, the other player must play 3 cards. If any of those three cards are a face card (Jack, Queen, King, or Ace), then the rule restarts for that face card. Note, if the card played was King (two Kings in a row), then it's slappable. If the three cards played were 2-10, the person who played the King gets the main deck.

–If a player plays an Ace, the other player must play 4 cards. If any of those four cards are a face card (Jack, Queen, King, or Ace), then the rule restarts for that face card. Note, if the card played was an Ace (two Aces in a row), then it's slappable. If the four cards played were 2-10, the person who played the Ace gets the main deck.

–Player loses when he or she runs out of cards to play. The loss doesn't trigger until after that turn plays out. (For example, that player could win a slap to get cards again, if there are more than 2 players playing.)



Common Questions

Q. How many players?

A. 2 players are supported for this version of the game.

Q. How do you "slap" and how do you play cards.

A. You can click the "SLAP" button, or click the main deck.

Q. How do you play a card?

A. You press "PLAY CARD".
